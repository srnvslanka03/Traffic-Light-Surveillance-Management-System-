Signal basics

Red

Complete stop behind the stop line. Right-on-red only when explicitly allowed.

Penalty: ₹1,000

Yellow / Amber

Prepare to stop. Entering the intersection after amber starts invites challans.

Penalty: ₹1,000

Green

Proceed while yielding to emergency vehicles and pedestrians. Respect dedicated turn arrows.

Precaution: Scan zebra crossing

Right-of-way reminders

  • Always give way to pedestrians already on the zebra crossing.
  • Left lane is for overtaking/turning; keep to the left on multi-lane roads.
  • Emergency vehicles get priority irrespective of the signal state.
  • Two-wheelers should avoid lane splitting near the junction.
  • Use the dedicated bicycle box where provided before the stop line.

Penalties (per Motor Vehicles Act)

Signal jump

₹1,000 (cars) / ₹500 (two-wheelers) plus e-challan entry.

Wrong lane / dangerous driving

₹1,500 and/or license suspension.

Blocking pedestrian crossing

₹500 per offence plus demerit points.
